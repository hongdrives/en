ABOUT US - Ho Brother Bricks

HO BROTHER BRICKS SUPPLY SDN BHD (964692-A)

Started construction in 2011 and production in full swing since September 2012. Currently, our factory is producing 260,000 pieces per day using the latest Kiln Roasting Technology ( Internal combustion: 内燃烧砖) available to ensure best quality. Meanwhile, our products having SIRIM & Bomba Product Certification.

We work with small, medium, and large-sized businesses to help them either get started or expand into new territories. Whether it's a small bicycle shop opening its doors for the first time, or a large software company moving to a new headquarters, we've done it all and making sure everything went smoothly in the process.

COMMON CLAY BRICKS

Bricks are made from earth elements - clay, sand, minerals, etc. Just as these materials vary in their natural form, the end result after mixing processing and firing will produce variation in colours and sizes, even from the same batch (and more so from batch to batch). For this reason, technical specifications may exhibit slight variation from product to product and from test to test.
